Shadow & contour studies

Sometimes I pretend that whatever I'm drawing is transparent. Then, I draw the new shapes that the transparent object and its shadows make up, or at least the ones I find interesting. In drawings like these, I play with shadows and the shapes they create as they move.

These are all 9x12 and were done in ballpoint pen on copy paper. They are in order from oldest to newest, clockwise starting from the the top left, all done within December, 2005.
