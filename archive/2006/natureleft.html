Inspired by nature

With this series of work, which isn't necessarily connected in any other way than subject matter, I found an inspiration in nature, which I'm sure everyone has or will. What started as boring drawings of flowers and ficus trees evolved into some slightly less boring etchings, a couple of which I really like. All done from January - April, 2005, in order from oldest to newest.

(From top to bottom, left to right)
1. 18 x 24, black marker on newsprint
2. 5 x 12, pen & marker on tracing paper
3. 9 x 12, mixed media on watercolor paper
4. 9 x 12, hard & soft ground etching
5. 9 x 12, drypoint & soft ground etching
6. 9 x 12, aquatint
