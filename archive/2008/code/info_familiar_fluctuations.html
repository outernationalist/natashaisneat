Familiar Fluctuations of
Loops, Jabs, and Spears

May 2007
Randomly generated drawings

This series of drawings was created with custom software (written in Processing) that subtly manipulated a source shape into infinite variations. It explores the formal effects of continuity and discontinuity in rhythm and symmetry.

This piece was included in the VOID exhibition curated by Tiffany Holmes and Matt Nelson, at GARDENfresh Gallery in Chicago.
