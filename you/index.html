Make Your Own

  1. Download the Adobe Photoshop source file.
  2. Make it your own by playing around with the bevel and outer glow layer styles, choosing a background, and adding a flourish.
  3. Print it out really big.
  4. Hang it somewhere you can see each day. You are amazing!
